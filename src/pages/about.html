About Us

Your Local Home Improvement Experts

Traverse City Handy is a locally owned and operated handyman service dedicated to providing professional, on-time, and skilled craftsmanship for homes and businesses across Traverse City, Michigan. We specialize in a wide range of services, including custom carpentry, bath and kitchen remodels, interior and exterior painting, and detailed trim work.

Our mission is to bring your home improvement vision to life with reliability and expertise. We handle every project with meticulous care, ensuring quality results that last. From start to finish, our educated and skilled team works efficiently to complete your project on schedule, with a commitment to 100% customer satisfaction.

I'm Dean Borland, founder of Traverse City Handy. I believe in a straightforward approach to home improvement. We are dedicated to providing clear communication, transparent pricing, and truly professional results. We take pride in our work and are committed to being on-time and educated on the latest techniques and materials. Let us show you what quality craftsmanship looks like.
